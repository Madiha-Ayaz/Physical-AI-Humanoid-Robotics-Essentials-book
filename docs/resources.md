## Books

### Robotics Fundamentals
- **"Robotics, Vision and Control"** by Peter Corke - Mathematical foundations
- **"Probabilistic Robotics"** by Thrun, Burgard, Fox - SLAM, localization, mapping
- **"Modern Robotics"** by Lynch & Park - Kinematics, dynamics, control
- **"Introduction to Autonomous Mobile Robots"** by Siegwart & Nourbakhsh

### ROS-Specific
- **"A Gentle Introduction to ROS"** by Jason O'Kane (free PDF)
- **"Programming Robots with ROS"** by Quigley, Gerkey, Smart - O'Reilly
- **"ROS Robotics Projects"** by Lentin Joseph - Packt Publishing
- **"Mastering ROS for Robotics Programming"** by Lentin Joseph & Jonathan Cacace

### AI & Machine Learning
- **"Deep Learning"** by Goodfellow, Bengio, Courville - Foundations
- **"Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction"** by Sutton & Barto - RL theory
- **"Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn, Keras, and TensorFlow"** by Aurélien Géron
